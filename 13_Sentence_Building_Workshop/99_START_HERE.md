## Your Daily 70-Minute Routine (No Exceptions)

### Morning (20 minutes) - Pattern Drills

**Monday-Friday:** Focus on the week's pattern type
- Week 1: SV, SVO, SLA, SVIO/DO, SVOC patterns
- Week 2: Compound and complex sentences
- Week 3: Advanced structures
- Week 4: Task-specific patterns

**Weekend:** Mix and review patterns from the week

**See:** Daily Drilling Schedule (Week-by-week breakdown provided)

---

### Afternoon (30 minutes) - Extended Writing

**Days 1-3:** Write 8 connected sentences on one topic  
**Days 4-5:** Write a short paragraph (10-15 sentences)  
**Weekends:** Light practice or full mini-task

**See:** Daily Drilling Schedule for Week 1-4 specifics

---

### Evening (20 minutes) - Review & Error Checking

**EVERY EVENING, 5-minute checklist:**

1. ✓ Does each sentence have a clear subject and verb?
2. ✓ Does the verb match the subject?
3. ✓ Are all verbs in the same timeframe (not mixed past/present)?
4. ✓ Does each sentence feel complete (not a fragment)?
5. ✓ Did I use adjectives after linking verbs and adverbs after action verbs?

**Then (10 minutes):** Log any errors in your Mistake Log (provided in Daily Drilling Schedule)

## Your Week-by-Week Progression

### Week 1: Foundation (Band 4-5)

**Goal:** Master the 5 basic sentence patterns until they feel automatic.

**Learn:** [02_Band3_to_Band5_Foundations.md](02_Band3_to_Band5_Foundations.md)

**The 5 Patterns:**
1. Subject + Verb (SV) → "Prices fell."
2. Subject + Verb + Object (SVO) → "The government raised awareness."
3. Subject + Linking Verb + Adjective/Noun (SLA) → "The data is reliable."
4. Subject + Verb + Indirect + Direct Object (SVIO/DO) → "I gave him a book."
5. Subject + Verb + Object + Complement (SVOC) → "I found the movie interesting."

**By end of Week 1:**
- [ ] Can write 60+ sentences using these 5 patterns with 90% accuracy
- [ ] No subject-verb agreement errors
- [ ] No sentence fragments
- [ ] Can identify pattern misuse in your own writing

**Expected Writing Level:** Band 4-5

---

### Week 2: Expansion (Band 5-6)

**Goal:** Learn to combine sentences. Create variety.

**Learn:** [03_Band5_to_Band7_Intermediate.md](03_Band5_to_Band7_Intermediate.md)

**New Skills:**
- Compound sentences (with and, but, or)
- Complex sentences (with because, when, if, while, although)
- Subordination (dependent vs. independent clauses)
- Punctuation (when to use commas, semicolons)

**By end of Week 2:**
- [ ] Can write compound sentences without comma splices
- [ ] Can write complex sentences with correct subordination
- [ ] Can vary sentence length for emphasis
- [ ] Can write 150-word paragraph with good structure

**Expected Writing Level:** Band 5-6

---

### Week 3: Sophistication (Band 6-7)

**Goal:** Add advanced structures and sophisticated word choices.

**Learn:** [04_Band7_to_Band9_Advanced.md](04_Band7_to_Band9_Advanced.md)

**New Skills:**
- Noun phrases (advanced descriptions)
- Participial phrases (using -ing and -ed forms)
- Inversion (for emphasis)
- Nominalization (verbs → nouns)
- Sophisticated vocabulary and collocations

**By end of Week 3:**
- [ ] Can use advanced structures naturally
- [ ] Sentences sound sophisticated (not simple or choppy)
- [ ] Vocabulary is appropriate for Band 8-9
- [ ] Can write 150-word IELTS Task 1 paragraph with accuracy

**Expected Writing Level:** Band 6-7

---

### Week 4: Mastery (Band 8-9)

**Goal:** Integrate all skills. Write full IELTS tasks with Band 9 accuracy.

**Learn:** [01_Sentence_Patterns_Mastery.md](01_Sentence_Patterns_Mastery.md) (review + polish)

**Focus:**
- Task 1: Describing data, trends, comparisons (150 words)
- Task 2: Body paragraphs with arguments and examples (250 words max)
- Fluency and naturalness
- Rare grammar errors (1-2 max per task)

**By end of Week 4:**
- [ ] Can write complete Task 1 (150 words) with Band 9 accuracy
- [ ] Can write complete Task 2 body paragraph (150+ words) with Band 8-9 accuracy
- [ ] Sentences flow naturally (not formulaic)
- [ ] Grammar is nearly flawless

**Expected Writing Level:** Band 8-9
