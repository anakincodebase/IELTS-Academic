## Quick Self-Assessment: What's Your Current Writing Level?

**Band 3-4 (Very Basic):**
- Sentences are short and choppy
- Many simple present tense only
- Rarely use complex structures
- Frequent grammar errors (subject-verb agreement, tense consistency)
- Example: "I go to school. I study English. English is hard."

**Band 5 (Elementary):**
- Mix of simple and compound sentences
- Some past tense usage
- Basic connecting words (and, but, because)
- Grammar mostly correct but formulaic
- Example: "I go to school and I study English, but it is very hard because I don't practice enough."

**Band 6-7 (Intermediate):**
- Complex sentences with subordinate clauses
- Good variety of tenses
- Sophisticated vocabulary sometimes
- Mostly accurate grammar with occasional errors
- Example: "Although I study English regularly, it remains challenging because I lack sufficient practice opportunities, which affects my overall proficiency."

**Band 8-9 (Advanced):**
- Sophisticated complex structures (noun phrases, embedded clauses, inversion)
- Natural vocabulary choices (synonyms, collocations)
- Flawless grammar and punctuation
- Varied sentence length for emphasis
- Example: "Despite my regular engagement with English, the language poses persistent challenges, primarily owing to insufficient immersive practice—a deficit that has substantially impacted my communicative competence."

## What You'll Learn

### Week 1: Building Blocks
- ✓ Simple sentence structure (subject + verb + object)

- ✓ Basic punctuation (periods, commas, apostrophes)
- ✓ Tense basics (simple present, simple past, simple future)

### Week 2: Expansion
- ✓ Compound sentences (joining with and, but, or)
- ✓ Complex sentences (with because, when, if, while)
- ✓ Coordinating vs. subordinating conjunctions
- ✓ Variety in sentence length and structure

### Week 3: Sophistication
- ✓ Advanced complex structures (noun phrases, participial phrases)
- ✓ Inversion for emphasis
- ✓ Nominalization (turning verbs into nouns)
- ✓ Sophisticated vocabulary and word choice

### Week 4: Mastery & Integration
- ✓ Fluency and naturalness
- ✓ Task-specific sentence structures (Task 1 vs. Task 2)
- ✓ Polishing and refinement
- ✓ Writing full essays with Band 9 accuracy
