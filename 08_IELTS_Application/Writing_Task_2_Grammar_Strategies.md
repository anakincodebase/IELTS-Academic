# Writing Task 2 Grammar Strategies

## Core Objective
> [!info] Rule
> Band 7+ writing requires consistent accuracy plus a clear variety of complex sentence structures.

## 1) Grammar Mix to Target
Use a controlled mix:
- Present Simple for facts and claims
- Present Perfect for modern trends
- Modals for hedging and recommendations
- Complex sentences for reasoning

> [!example] IELTS Example
> In recent years, online education has expanded rapidly, and this trend may reshape traditional classrooms.

## 2) Build Body Paragraphs with Controlled Structures
Recommended sequence:
- Topic sentence (complex)
- Explanation (cause/effect)
- Example (relative clause)
- Link-back sentence (result/concession)

Useful patterns:
- Although X, Y.
- Because X, Y.
- If X, Y.
- Not only X but also Y.

## 3) Use Hedging Instead of Absolute Claims
Replace rigid statements with balanced grammar:
- can, could, may, might, would
- appears to, tends to, is likely to

> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Absolute: This policy solves poverty.
>
> Better: This policy could help reduce poverty in urban areas.

## 4) Upgrade Linking Grammar
Use logic-based connectors:
- Cause: because, since, given that
- Contrast: although, whereas, however
- Result: therefore, consequently, as a result

> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Avoid comma splice: X, therefore Y.
>
> Use: X; therefore, Y. or X. Therefore, Y.

## 5) High-Value Grammar Features
- Relative clauses: policies that..., students who...
- Conditional forms: if governments invest..., outcomes will improve
- Occasional inversion for advanced control: Not only does this approach..., but it also...

## 6) Error Traps That Lower Scores
> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Subject-verb mismatch: Everyone have...
>
> Correct: Everyone has...

> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Countability error: many informations
>
> Correct: much information

> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Informal forms: gonna, wanna, kids in formal argumentation

## 90-Second Final Check
- One clear tense system used logically?
- At least 2 to 3 complex sentences per body paragraph?
- Modal hedging present?
- Preposition and article errors removed?
