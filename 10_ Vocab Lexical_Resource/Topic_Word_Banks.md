# Topic Word Banks

## How to Use This Note
1. Pick one topic before writing.
2. Select 5 to 7 words and collocations only.
3. Use at least 3 of them accurately in one paragraph.
4. Reuse only words you can pronounce and spell confidently.

## 1) Education
| Function | High-Value Words and Collocations |
|---|---|
| policy | curriculum reform, assessment criteria, learning outcomes |
| quality | instructional quality, teacher training, classroom engagement |
| equity | equal access, disadvantaged learners, digital divide |

## Essay-Type Mapping
- Opinion essay: use claim + cause + consequence collocations.
- Advantages vs disadvantages: split vocabulary into benefit and drawback sets.
- Problem-solution: use issue and intervention collocations in separate paragraphs.
- Discuss both views: assign one lexical set to each viewpoint.
