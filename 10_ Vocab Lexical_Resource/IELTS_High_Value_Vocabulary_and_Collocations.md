|---|---|---|
| prone (adj) | likely to do/experience | prone to errors, prone to making mistakes |
| accountable (adj) | responsible for actions | held accountable, accountable to the public |

| fundamental (adj) | essential, primary | fundamental right, fundamental change |

| proportion (n) | part of a whole; percentage | large proportion, certain proportion |

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- Prefer accurate simple alternatives over risky advanced words.
- Use collocations as chunks instead of translating word-by-word.
- Check spelling for high-risk items: phenomenon, deficiency, irrespective, detrimental.

## Quick Practice
1. Pick 10 words from one section.
2. Write 5 Task 2 sentences with correct collocations.
3. Reuse 3 words in Speaking Part 3 answers.
