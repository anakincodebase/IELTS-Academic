# Change Growth and Solutions Synonyms

## Core Mapping (IELTS-Safe)
| Core Word | Better Alternatives | Context Rule |
|---|---|---|
| develop | cultivate, foster | Foster often means encourage growth. |
| increase | boost, raise | Raise is often used for levels/rates/funds. |
| improve | enhance, ameliorate | Ameliorate is formal and spelling-sensitive. |
| create | generate, establish | Establish suits long-term systems/institutions. |
| help | assist, aid | Aid is formal; often used as aid in + noun/gerund. |
| provide | supply, offer | Supply fulfills a practical need; offer makes available. |
| solve (solution quality) | feasible, viable | Feasible = practical to do; viable = can work successfully. |

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct safe substitutions
- develop -> foster
- increase -> boost
- improve -> enhance
- create -> generate
- help -> assist
- provide -> offer, supply

### Context-dependent substitutions
- develop -> cultivate (skills/habits over time)
- create -> establish (long-term structure)
- help -> aid in (formal structure)
- improve -> ameliorate (advanced formal style)

### Use carefully
- ameliorate: correct meaning and spelling are essential.
- viable/feasible: use for evaluating solutions, not as direct verbs.

## Example Sentences
- Governments should foster innovation through targeted research funding.
- Better teacher training can boost classroom performance.
- Community programs can enhance social cohesion in large cities.
- New regulations may generate safer working conditions.
- This proposal is feasible in the short term and viable in the long term.
