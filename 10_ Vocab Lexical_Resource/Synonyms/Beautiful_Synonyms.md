### Related words (context-dependent)
- pretty, gorgeous, radiant, captivating, alluring
- aesthetic, polished, delightful, scenic, handsome

### Not direct synonyms of beautiful (use carefully)
- expensive (price, not appearance)
- modern (time/style, not always beauty)
- clean (hygiene, not beauty)
- famous (popularity, not beauty)
- smart (intelligence/tech style, not beauty)

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- beautiful, attractive = general and safe
- elegant, refined, sophisticated = formal and strong for writing
- stunning, breathtaking = stronger emotional tone; use when clearly justified
- picturesque and scenic = best for places and natural landscapes

Extra guidance:
- In Task 1 map descriptions, attractive and well-designed are often safer than very emotional words.
- In Task 2, explain why something is beautiful (layout, symmetry, color, environment).

## Example Sentences
- The renovated town center is more attractive and pedestrian-friendly.
- The coastline offers a picturesque view that draws many tourists.
- The new library has an elegant design with open study spaces.
- The museum building is striking because of its modern glass facade.
- The park became more appealing after the landscaping project.
- The village remains beautiful despite rapid urban expansion nearby.
- The interior is refined and well-organized, which improves user experience.
