## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct rich synonyms (safe in most IELTS contexts)
- rich
- wealthy
- affluent
- prosperous
- well-off
- financially secure
- high-income
- resource-rich
- high-earning
- solvent
- comfortable

### Related words (context-dependent)
- prosperous, resource-rich (when describing economies/countries)
- thriving, flourishing (economic performance, not personal wealth)
- abundant, plentiful (quantity, not monetary wealth)
- economically strong, advantaged, upscale (socioeconomic framing)
- opulent, luxurious, deep-pocketed, moneyed, elite
- successful, premium, privileged, prosperous-looking

### Not direct synonyms of rich (use carefully)
- big (size, not wealth)
- educated (education, not wealth)
- powerful (authority, not wealth)
- lucky (chance, not wealth)
- modern (time/style, not wealth)

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- rich, wealthy, affluent = safe general terms
- prosperous suits countries, regions, and economies
- resource-rich is best for natural-resource contexts
- opulent and luxurious are stronger style words for visible wealth

Extra guidance:
- For formal writing, use wealth distribution, high-income groups, and economically advantaged communities.
- Avoid informal slang like loaded in Task 2.

## Example Sentences
- Wealthy households can often access better private education.
- The region became more prosperous after industrial investment.
- Some countries are resource-rich but still face inequality.
- Affluent neighborhoods usually receive better public services.
- Economic growth does not always benefit low-income groups equally.
- The city has a thriving business sector and high tax revenue.
- A financially secure population tends to spend more on healthcare.
