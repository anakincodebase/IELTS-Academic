# Abstract Concepts and Adjectives Synonyms

## Core Mapping (IELTS-Safe)
| Core Word | Better Alternatives | Context Rule |
|---|---|---|
| the environment | the natural world, ecosystems, the planet | Ecosystems is scientific; the planet is global framing. |
| health | well-being, physical condition | Well-being includes mental/social dimensions. |
| life | existence, way of living, lifestyles | Existence is formal/abstract. |
| believe (verb) | argue, contend | Use when presenting reasoned claims. |
| different | various, diverse | Diverse suggests a broad range of distinct types. |
| social | communal, societal, public | Societal = society-wide formal meaning. |
| public (for everyone/state) | state-funded, state-run, civic, community | Civic links to citizenship duties. |
| personal | individual, private | Private = not for public access. |
| available | accessible, obtainable | Accessible also implies easy access/use. |
| general | overall, broadly | Use overall for summary judgments. |

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct safe substitutions
- environment -> the natural world
- health -> well-being
- different -> diverse, various
- personal -> private, individual
- available -> accessible
- general -> overall

### Context-dependent substitutions
- believe -> argue, contend (formal argument style)
- social -> societal (formal), communal (community-centered)
- public -> civic, state-run, state-funded
- life -> existence (abstract/philosophical contexts)

### Use carefully
- ecosystems is best in scientific/environment discussions.
- contend is formal and should be followed by a clear claim.

## Example Sentences
- Protecting the natural world requires long-term policy commitment.
- Public health planning should include mental well-being.
- Some scholars argue that urban lifestyles reduce community cohesion.
- The report presents various solutions for reducing household waste.
- Online services must be accessible to people with disabilities.
