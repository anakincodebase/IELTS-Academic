## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- disappointed = mild result-related sadness
- sorrowful, heartbroken = strong emotional loss
- depressed is often clinical; use carefully

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct sad synonyms (safe in most IELTS contexts)
- sad
- unhappy
- upset
- disappointed
- discouraged
- sorrowful
- miserable
- heartbroken
- downcast
- dejected

### Related words (context-dependent)
- gloomy, melancholic/melancholy, mournful, somber, morose, glum
- anguish, grief, sorrow, despair, heartbreak (mostly nouns)
- mourn, grieve, lament (verbs)

### Use carefully
- depressed (often clinical/mental-health context)
- inconsolable, grief-stricken (very strong, specific situations)

## Example Sentences
- Many young people feel disappointed when jobs are unavailable.
- The community was sorrowful after the natural disaster.
- Some students become discouraged after repeated mock-test failures.
- He felt downcast after missing the scholarship deadline.
- Public frustration turned into despair during the prolonged crisis.
