## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- ridicule, mock = strong and emotional
- undermine, dismiss, downplay = common in academic arguments

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct belittling verbs (safe in most IELTS contexts)
- belittle
- demean
- disparage
- denigrate
- ridicule
- mock
- trivialize
- dismiss

### Related reducing-impact verbs (context-dependent)
- downplay
- minimize
- understate
- brush aside
- write off
- undermine

### Use carefully
- insult, sneer at, scoff at, patronize (strong tone, often interpersonal)

## Example Sentences
- Some critics dismiss the long-term effects of air pollution.
- Media narratives can downplay rural poverty.
- It is unfair to belittle vocational education pathways.
- The spokesperson attempted to minimize the severity of the crisis.
- Policymakers should not write off community-level solutions.
