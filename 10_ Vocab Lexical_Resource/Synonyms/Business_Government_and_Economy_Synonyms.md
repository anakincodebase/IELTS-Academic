# Business Government and Economy Synonyms

## Core Mapping (IELTS-Safe)
| Core Word | Better Alternatives | Context Rule |
|---|---|---|
| government | the authorities, the state | The state is formal and policy-heavy. |
| work (noun) | employment, career | Career implies long-term professional path. |
| companies | corporations, businesses | Corporations often suggests large firms. |
| economic | financial, monetary | Monetary is specific to money supply/rates. |

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- Use authorities for neutral references to power holders.
- Use employment when discussing labor markets and job access.
- Use career for progression, ambition, and long-term planning.
- Use monetary mostly for inflation, central banks, or interest rates.

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct safe substitutions
- government -> the authorities, the state
- work (noun) -> employment
- companies -> businesses
- economic -> financial

### Context-dependent substitutions
- companies -> corporations (large-scale contexts)
- work (noun) -> career (long-term pathway)
- economic -> monetary (macroeconomic policy only)

## Example Sentences
- The authorities should regulate misleading online advertisements.
- Stable employment improves household security.
- Large corporations can create jobs but also increase market pressure on small businesses.
- Monetary policy can influence borrowing and investment behavior.
