## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- repeal = remove a law
- rescind/revoke = formally cancel a decision or permission
- retract = take back a statement

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct cancel/withdraw verbs (safe in most IELTS contexts)
- cancel
- call off
- withdraw
- revoke
- rescind
- repeal
- terminate
- discontinue
- retract

### Related action words (context-dependent)
- annul
- nullify
- abort
- suspend
- shelve
- scrap
- abandon
- pull out of

### Use carefully
- suspend (usually temporary)
- reverse (change direction; not always full cancellation)
- drop (informal in some contexts)

## Example Sentences
- The government repealed the outdated regulation.
- The organizer called off the event due to safety concerns.
- The company withdrew its proposal after public criticism.
- The ministry rescinded the order after legal review.
- The board decided to suspend the policy temporarily.
