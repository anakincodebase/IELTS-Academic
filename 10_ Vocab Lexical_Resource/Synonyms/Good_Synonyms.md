### Related words (context-dependent)
- outstanding, exceptional, remarkable, superior, impressive
- commendable, competent, productive, optimum, decent

### Not direct synonyms of good (use carefully)
- lucky (chance, not quality)
- rich (wealth, not quality)
- big (size, not quality)
- easy (difficulty level, not quality)
- popular (public preference, not always quality)

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- good = basic and natural; do not remove it completely
- effective, beneficial, practical = high-value formal options
- outstanding, exceptional = very strong; reserve for clear extremes
- reliable and robust are excellent for systems and policy discussion

Extra guidance:
- In Task 2, precise pairs often score better: effective policy, beneficial outcome, practical measure.
- Replace repeated good with context-fit words, not random advanced terms.

## Example Sentences
- Public transport is a good solution for reducing congestion.
- Early intervention can be highly beneficial for children's development.
- The policy produced effective results within two years.
- The school has a reliable support system for struggling students.
- A practical approach is often better than an idealistic one.
- The program delivered strong outcomes in rural communities.
- The final proposal was constructive and financially sound.
