## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- authority = respected top-level expert in a field
- proficient, competent = strong practical ability

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct expert-level words (safe in most IELTS contexts)
- expert
- specialist
- professional
- skilled
- proficient
- competent
- experienced
- knowledgeable

### Related high-skill words (context-dependent)
- adept
- accomplished
- practitioner
- consultant
- analyst
- technician

### Use carefully
- maestro, virtuoso, connoisseur (domain-specific, often arts/taste)
- authority (can refer to institution as well as person)

## Example Sentences
- A leading expert warned that the policy lacked long-term planning.
- Skilled technicians are essential for renewable-energy expansion.
- The report was reviewed by experienced professionals.
- The committee consulted several specialists before finalizing the policy.
- Competent practitioners can reduce implementation errors.
