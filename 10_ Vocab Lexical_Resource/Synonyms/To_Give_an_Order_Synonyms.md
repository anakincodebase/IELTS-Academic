## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- instruct, direct = procedural/neutral
- mandate, decree = legal or authority context
- enforce = apply a rule, not just announce it

## Accuracy Filter for IELTS
### Direct order/command verbs (safe in most IELTS contexts)
- order
- command
- instruct
- direct
- require
- mandate
- compel
- demand

### Related authority-action verbs (context-dependent)
- decree
- dictate
- stipulate
- impose
- prescribe
- authorize

### Use carefully
- request, urge (softer than order)
- enforce (implementation stage, not initial command)

## Example Sentences
- The authority instructed residents to evacuate immediately.
- New regulations mandate helmet use for all riders.
- The court ordered the company to pay compensation.
- The agency required all factories to install filters.
- The ministry directed schools to update safety procedures.
