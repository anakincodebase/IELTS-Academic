### Related words (context-dependent)
- practical, transparent, effortless, smooth, quick, efficient, low-effort
- low-risk, controllable, navigable, easy-to-maintain

### Not direct synonyms of easy (use carefully)
- good (quality, not difficulty)
- cheap (price, not difficulty)
- fast (speed, not always ease)
- short (length, not ease)
- popular (preference, not ease)

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- easy and simple are common and safe
- straightforward and manageable are strong formal alternatives
- feasible is best when discussing plans and policy practicality
- effortless should be used carefully because most tasks are not truly effortless

Extra guidance:
- Explain why something is easy: clear steps, low cost, available support.
- In Task 2, practical and feasible often sound more academic than easy.

## Example Sentences
- The application process is simple and easy to follow.
- Public transport should be accessible for elderly citizens.
- The new system is straightforward and user-friendly.
- For beginners, this method is manageable and low-risk.
- The proposal is feasible within current budget constraints.
- Online payment has made daily transactions more convenient.
- The instructions are clear-cut, so implementation is easier.
