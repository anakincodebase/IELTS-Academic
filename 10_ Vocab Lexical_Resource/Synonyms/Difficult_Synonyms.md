### Related words (context-dependent)
- intricate, laborious, burdensome, strenuous, exhausting
- awkward, confusing, uphill, obstacle-filled, severe

### Not direct synonyms of difficult (use carefully)
- bad (quality, not difficulty)
- slow (speed, not difficulty)
- expensive (cost, not difficulty)
- impossible (absolute impossibility)
- dangerous (risk, not necessarily difficulty)

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- difficult and challenging are safe defaults
- complex and complicated suit systems and policy issues
- daunting and formidable show stronger challenge
- use high-pressure and resource-intensive for formal task discussion

Extra guidance:
- Differentiate difficulty types: technical, financial, social, administrative.
- Add reason clauses to prove the challenge.

## Example Sentences
- It is difficult to implement reform without public support.
- Managing a large city is a complex administrative task.
- Climate migration presents a challenging policy dilemma.
- The transition was time-consuming but necessary.
- For small firms, compliance can be resource-intensive.
- Rural healthcare delivery remains a formidable challenge.
- Many graduates find it hard to secure stable employment initially.
