### Related words (context-dependent)
- needy, destitute, penniless, insolvent
- excluded, deprived-of-opportunity, minimal-resource, scarce

### Not direct synonyms of poor (use carefully)
- bad (quality, not economic status)
- small (size, not poverty)
- simple (complexity, not income)
- rural (location, not poverty)
- old (age, not income)

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- poor and low-income are neutral and safe in IELTS
- underprivileged and disadvantaged are strong for social-policy writing
- destitute and penniless are extreme and should be used sparingly
- avoid language that sounds disrespectful or absolute

Extra guidance:
- Use precise combinations: low-income households, disadvantaged communities, financially insecure workers.
- Clarify causes and effects when discussing poverty.

## Example Sentences
- Low-income families are more affected by rising food prices.
- Many underprivileged students lack access to digital tools.
- The policy aims to support economically weak communities.
- Rural clinics often serve resource-poor populations.
- Financially insecure workers face greater housing stress.
- Poverty can limit educational opportunity across generations.
- Targeted subsidies can protect vulnerable households.
