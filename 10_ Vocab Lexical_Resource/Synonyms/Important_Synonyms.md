- high-priority
- far-reaching

### Related words (context-dependent)
- influential, notable, prominent, meaningful, valuable
- decisive, indispensable, substantial, weighty, serious

### Not direct synonyms of important (use carefully)
- famous (popularity, not importance)
- big (size, not importance)
- difficult (complexity, not importance)
- rich (wealth, not importance)
- new (recency, not importance)

## IELTS-Safe Usage Notes
- important and significant are the safest universal options
- crucial, vital, essential = stronger urgency
- strategic and pivotal are excellent in policy/economy essays
- urgent and pressing are best for immediate social issues

Extra guidance:
- Vary by noun: crucial issue, significant trend, key factor, central argument.
- Do not overuse strongest words in every sentence.

## Example Sentences
- Education plays an important role in long-term development.
- Public trust is a crucial factor in policy success.
- Clean water remains an essential service in rural regions.
- This issue has far-reaching consequences for future generations.
- Digital literacy is becoming increasingly significant in modern labor markets.
- Affordable transport is a key requirement for social mobility.
- Climate adaptation is now a high-priority national objective.
