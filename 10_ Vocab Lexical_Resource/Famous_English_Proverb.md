# Most Useful English Proverbs

- **1. If you play with fire, you'll get burned**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > If you get involved in something dangerous or beyond your abilities, you will probably experience negative consequences.

- **2. If you want something done right, you have to do it yourself**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Don't depend on someone else to do a good job; do it yourself.

- **3. Knowledge is power**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > The more you know, the more powerful you can be in different areas of your life.

- **4. Laughter is the best medicine**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > When you're in a difficult situation, laughing can make it easier to get through that situation.

- **5. No man is an island**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > No one is truly capable of living alone. We need human connection to be healthy.

- **6. Actions speak louder than words**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > What you do is more important than what you say.

- **7. All good things must come to an end**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Everything ends; good times don't last forever.

- **8. A picture is worth a thousand words**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > An image can tell a story better than words.

- **9. Beauty is in the eye of the beholder**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > What is "beautiful" is different for everyone.

- **10. Better late than never**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > It's better to finish something late than to never do it at all.

- **11. Birds of a feather flock together**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > People who are similar spend time together.

- **12. Don't judge a book by its cover**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Don't judge someone or something by appearance alone.

- **13. Don't count your chickens before they hatch**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Don't expect a positive result before you actually see it.

- **14. Don't put all of your eggs in one basket**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Don't put all of your hopes and resources into one goal or dream.

- **15. Don't put too many irons in the fire**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Don't try to do too many things at the same time; focus on one thing at a time.

- **16. Good things come to those who wait**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > If you are patient, good things can happen.

- **17. Honesty is the best policy**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > It's always better to tell the truth than it is to lie.

- **18. There is no place like home**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Your home is the most comfortable place in the world.

- **19. Easy come, easy go**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > When you get money quickly, like by winning it, it's easy to spend it or lose it quickly as well.

- **20. Don't bite the hand that feeds you**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > If someone is paying you or helping you out, you have to be careful not to make them angry or say bad things about them.

- **21. If you can't beat 'em, join 'em**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > When you try to change someone's behavior and it doesn't work, you might have to change your own instead.

- **22. One man's trash is another man's treasure**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Different people have different ideas about what is valuable.

- **23. There's no time like the present**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > If you need to do something, don't wait until later. Do it now.

- **24. Necessity is the mother of invention**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > When you're really in need, you think of creative solutions to your problems.

- **25. A penny saved is a penny earned**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Save your money.

- **26. Familiarity breeds contempt**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > When you're around someone for too long, you can get tired of them and annoyed by them.

- **27. You can't judge a book by its cover**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Things sometimes look different than they really are. A restaurant that looks old and small might have amazing food.

- **28. Good things come to those who wait**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Be patient.

- **29. Better late than never**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > The delayed occurrence or achievement of something that one desires is better than it not happening at all.

- **30. Birds of a feather flock together**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > People like to spend time with others who are similar to them.

- **31. Keep your friends close and your enemies closer**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > If you have an enemy, pretend to be friends with them instead of openly fighting with them. That way you can watch them carefully and figure out what they're planning.

- **32. A picture is worth a thousand words**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Pictures convey emotions and messages better than written or spoken explanations.

- **33. There's no such thing as a free lunch**
  > [!info] Meaning
  > Things that are offered for free always have a hidden cost.
