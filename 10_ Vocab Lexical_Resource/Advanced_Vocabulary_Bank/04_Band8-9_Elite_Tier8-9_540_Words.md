## Master Practice for Tier 9

**Method: Synonym Substitution Drill**

Take any IELTS reading passage. Pick one sentence:
"The company's rapid growth led to significant expansion."

**Replace with synonyms from families:**
- "The company's **burgeoning** success resulted in **considerable** proliferation."
- "The company's **escalating** trajectory precipitated **substantial** augmentation."
- "The company's **mushrooming** trajectory catalyzed **major** expansion."

**Daily Practice (20 minutes):**
1. Read one reading passage
2. Identify 5 key sentences
3. Rewrite each using different synonyms from these families
4. Check if your version sounds natural

**Result after 2 weeks:**
- You'll recognize ANY synonym instantly in reading
- Your writing vocabulary will improve dramatically
- Your paraphrasing speed will triple
