# 🎯 The 1,000-Word IELTS Matrix: Patch 1 (Words 1-50)

> [!success] **THE UPGRADE: WHY TYPES & COLLOCATIONS MATTER**
> 🧠 **Word Types:** Knowing if a word is a noun (n), verb (v), or adjective (adj) helps you predict answers in Listening summary completions.
> 🔗 **Word Family & Collocations (Variety):** IELTS rarely uses the exact same word form. If the text says "empirically proven," the question might ask for "empirical data." Collocations are words that naturally go together.
