## VOCABULARY DISTRIBUTION

### Band 7 Vocabulary (300 words)
- Academic Arguments (cogent, salient, nuance, substantiate, etc.)
- Problems & Solutions (exacerbate, mitigate, rectify, alleviate, etc.)
- Change & Progress (burgeoning, proliferate, transcend, ameliorate, etc.)
- Relationships (synergy, correlation, convergence, divergence, etc.)
- **Best for:** Writing Task 2, General Reading

### Band 8 Vocabulary (350 words)
- Strength & Impact (robust, paramount, fortify, formidable, potent, etc.)
- Judgment & Values (venerable, benign, heinous, magnanimous, venal, etc.)
- Environment & Social (sustainability, biodiversity, deforestation, emissions, etc.)
- Economic & Business (commodity, volatility, fiscal, deficit, lucrative, monopoly, etc.)
- Quality & Precision (meticulous, rigorous, acerbic, exacting, pedantic, etc.)
- **Best for:** Advanced Writing, Complex Reading, Speaking

### Band 9 Vocabulary (200 words)
- Complex Ideas & Abstract Concepts (paradigm, seminal, esoteric, pragmatic, etc.)
- Advanced Relationships (dichotomy, dialectic, synthesis, juxtaposition, etc.)
- Psychology & Human Behavior (apathy, complacency, propensity, inherent, etc.)
- Language & Communication (rhetoric, eloquent, cryptic, verbose, pedantic, etc.)
- Growth & Development (burgeoning, ardent, vivacious, etc.)
- Doubt & Certainty (equivocal, indubitable, spurious, irrefutable, credible, etc.)
- **Best for:** Academic Discussion, Complex Analysis, Expert communication

## STUDY TIPS

> **💡 PRO TIP:** Don't memorize. UNDERSTAND each word's:
> - Definition (what it means)
> - Mnemonic (memory trick)
> - Examples (how it's used in IELTS)
> - Synonyms (related words)

> **🎯 BEST PRACTICE:**
> 1. Read definition + mnemonic
> 2. Create own example sentence
> 3. Use in speaking practice
> 4. Use in writing practice
> 5. Teach someone else about it

> **📊 EFFECTIVENESS:**
> - Reading file once: 30% retention
> - Reading + creating examples: 70% retention
> - Reading + examples + active use: 90% retention
