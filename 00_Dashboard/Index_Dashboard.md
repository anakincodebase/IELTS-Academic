## How to Use This Vault

Each note is self-contained but linked to related concepts. When you're learning about simple sentences, you'll find links to more advanced structures, common errors, and real writing examples.

**Read the summary first.** Most notes start with a clear explanation of the concept. The examples come second. Drills and detailed rules come last—use those when you're ready to go deeper.

**Follow the examples.** IELTS rewards English that sounds natural, not exotic. Every example in this vault is written the way educated native speakers actually write.

**Know your weakness.** Don't study everything. If you score Band 7 on grammar, don't spend weeks on basic subject-verb agreement. Study the specific error patterns you make repeatedly.

## What Works at Band 8

Three things separate Band 8 from Band 6:

1. **Accuracy under pressure** — You make zero careless errors even when writing fast
2. **Sophisticated variety** — You move between simple, compound, and complex structures naturally
3. **Precise vocabulary** — You use the right word for the right effect, with correct collocations

This vault teaches all three. Pick your path and begin.
