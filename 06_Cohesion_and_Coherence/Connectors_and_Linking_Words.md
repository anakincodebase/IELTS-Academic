# <span style="color: #1a3a52">Connectors and Linking Words</span>

## <span style="color: #00838f">Core Purpose</span>

> [!info] Rule
> Connectors signal logic between ideas: addition, contrast, cause, effect, example, concession, and conclusion.

## <span style="color: #1b5e20">Functional Connector Bank</span>

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Addition</span>

- moreover, furthermore, in addition, additionally

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Contrast</span>

- however, in contrast, by contrast, conversely, whereas, while

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Cause and reason</span>

- because, since, given that, due to, owing to

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Effect and result</span>

- therefore, consequently, as a result, thus, hence

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Example and evidence</span>

- for example, for instance, to illustrate, a clear example is

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Concession</span>

- although, even though, despite this, nevertheless, nonetheless

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Sequencing and progression</span>

- initially, subsequently, meanwhile, eventually, finally

### <span style="color: #1b5e20">Conclusion</span>

- in conclusion, to sum up, overall, all in all

## <span style="color: #00838f">Connector Strength Ladder</span>

- basic: and, but, so
- mid: however, therefore, for example
- advanced-natural: by contrast, consequently, nevertheless, given that

> [!info] Rule
> High band comes from precise connector choice, not rare connector quantity.

## <span style="color: #d4af37">Anti-Repetition Strategy</span>

> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Repeating one connector family (especially moreover/furthermore) weakens cohesion and sounds formulaic.

Rotation strategy:

1. Use one high-signal connector per paragraph opening.
2. Use conjunction-based linking inside the paragraph.
3. Use reference chains with pronouns (this issue, this trend).

## <span style="color: #00838f">Punctuation Rules with Connectors</span>

### <span style="color: #00838f">Clause connector pattern</span>

- Although X, Y.
- Because X, Y.

### <span style="color: #00838f">Transition adverb pattern</span>

- X. However, Y.
- X; however, Y.

### <span style="color: #00838f">Noun phrase cause pattern</span>

- X occurred due to Y.
- X happened because of Y.

Wrong:

- X happened due to Y happened.

Correct:

- X happened because Y happened.

> [!example] IELTS Example
> Public transport remains underfunded. Consequently, lower-income residents face longer commute times.
> [!example] IELTS Example
> While digital tools improve access to learning, they can widen inequality when infrastructure is weak.

## <span style="color: #5e35b1">Task-Specific Connector Use</span>

### <span style="color: #5e35b1">Writing Task 1</span>

- comparison: while, whereas, in contrast, compared with
- sequence (process): first, next, after that, finally
- trend result: consequently, therefore

### <span style="color: #5e35b1">Writing Task 2</span>

- paragraph opening: however, moreover, by contrast
- claim support: because, given that, for example
- balanced close: nevertheless, despite this

### <span style="color: #5e35b1">Speaking Part 3</span>

- natural discourse links: generally, at the same time, on the other hand, in practice
- avoid over-formal essay connectors in speech

## <span style="color: #d4af37">Common Connector Errors</span>

- Comma splice:

❌ WRONG: The city expanded rail lines, however traffic still increased.
✅ RIGHT: The city expanded rail lines; however, traffic still increased.

- Logic mismatch:

❌ WRONG: Although this policy is expensive, therefore it should be expanded.
✅ RIGHT: Although this policy is expensive, it should be expanded.

- Over-formality with no meaning:
  Avoid stacking: Moreover, furthermore, in addition...

## <span style="color: #5e35b1">High-Score Tricks</span>

1. Connector pre-plan trick: Before writing, assign one connector family to each paragraph.
2. No-double-transition trick: Use either a transition adverb or a subordinating conjunction, not both in one short sentence.
3. Read-aloud logic test: If the connector sounds unnecessary when spoken, remove it.

## <span style="color: #5e35b1">5-Minute Drill</span>

1. Take one body paragraph draft.
2. Underline all connectors.
3. Delete 30% of them.
4. Keep only the ones that change logic clarity.
5. Recheck punctuation around remaining transitions.
