# Compound-Complex Sentences

## Definition
> [!info] Rule
> A compound-complex sentence includes at least two independent clauses and at least one dependent clause.

This structure is useful when you need to present contrast plus explanation in one sentence.

## Structure Model
Dependent clause + Independent clause 1 + coordinator + Independent clause 2.
![[Pasted image 20260412012002.png]]
## Examples
> [!example] IELTS Example
> Although the council introduced new bus lanes, commute times stayed high, and many workers continued using private cars.

> [!example] IELTS Example
> Because the power grid was outdated, outages became frequent, and small businesses lost revenue.

## Risks
> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Overloaded sentence risk: too many clauses can reduce clarity and increase grammar errors.

Use this structure selectively and keep verb agreement accurate.

- Use one compound-complex sentence near the end of a Task 2 body paragraph to synthesize argument + evidence.
