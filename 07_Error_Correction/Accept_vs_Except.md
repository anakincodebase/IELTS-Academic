# Accept vs Except

## Core Difference
> [!info] Rule
> accept is usually a verb meaning receive or agree to.
> except is usually a preposition/conjunction meaning excluding or apart from.

## Accept (Verb)
Meaning:
- to agree to receive
- to agree with a proposal or situation

Examples:
- I accepted the job offer.
- She accepted that the plan needed revision.

> [!example] IELTS Example
> The committee accepted the recommendation to expand public transport.

## Except (Preposition/Conjunction)
Meaning:
- excluding
- apart from

Examples:
- Everyone attended except Ali.
- I like all subjects except chemistry.

> [!example] IELTS Example
> All categories increased except public spending on libraries.

## High-Frequency Confusions
> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Incorrect: I except your opinion.
>
> Correct: I accept your opinion.

> [!warning] Band Penalty
> Incorrect: All students accept John were present.
>
> Correct: All students except John were present.

## Memory Trick
- accept = say yes, receive
- except = exclude

## IELTS Application
- Writing Task 1: use except for outlier categories in graph comparisons.
- Writing Task 2: use accept mainly for decisions, offers, arguments, or realities.
- Speaking: pause for one second before saying the word to choose meaning first.

## Mini Drill
Choose the correct word:
1. The agency ______ the new proposal.
2. Every city improved ______ one.
3. It is hard to ______ this explanation.

Answers:
1. accepted
2. except
3. accept
