## Purpose
This folder helps you use natural English in speaking without forcing advanced phrases.

## Band 8.5 Policy
1. Use idiomatic language sparingly and naturally.
2. Accuracy is more important than showing off difficult expressions.
3. If you are unsure, prefer clear plain English.

## Fast Study Flow
1. Learn 5 idioms from real context.
2. Practice 20 safe contractions out loud.
3. Add 5 British expressions that fit your real personality.
4. Record one Part 1 and one Part 3 answer.
5. Audit for naturalness and grammar accuracy.
